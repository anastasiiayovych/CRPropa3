Detailed Description

JF12FieldPlanck: the JF12 galactic magnetic field model with corrections suggested by the Planck Collaboration.

See: Planck Collaboration, "Planck intermediate results. XLII. Large-scale Galactic magnetic fields", A&A 596 (2016) A103; arXiv:1601.00546

This variant of the JF12 field uses only different parameters compared to the standard JF12 implementation.
